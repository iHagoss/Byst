Maintenance work

This tab contains the maintenance schedule for planning maintenance tasks and registering completed maintenance tasks on the bending machine.

Each pending maintenance task that has not been performed yet is identified by the icon.

NOTE:

The maintenance tasks displayed can only be a selection of all necessary maintenance work.

With regard to performing all maintenance work on the machine correctly, the instructions always apply that are given in the operating and maintenance instructions for the machine!

Confirming maintenance tasks
  1. Perform the pending maintenance tasks.
  2. Highlight the maintenance tasks on the Maintenance work tab.
  3. Press the Confirm button. The icon is removed from all selected maintenance tasks and replaced with a new maintenance date based on the maintenance interval.

Info: The maintenance icon appears again as soon as the maintenance date is reached.

Safety regulations
  • Maintenance staff must be familiar with the technical conditions of the cutting machine. They must have read and understood all instructions relating to operation and safety in these maintenance instructions.
  • The safety devices of the machineâ€™s danger zone that must not be entered while operating the machine can be looked up in the operating instructions.
  • The machine must be de-energized when performing maintenance and repair work. The main switch must be locked out with a padlock and the key must be kept inaccessible to third parties.
  • After completing maintenance and repair work, all covers and safety devices that were removed must be completely refitted before putting the machine back into operation.
Staff requirements

The owner of the machine is responsible for ensuring that operating, maintenance and repair staff are appropriately trained.

Optionally, various training courses are offered for the machines. For details, please contact the Customer Training Center.

Operating resources

When handling operating resources, particularly gear oil, solvents and greases, always observe the safety provisions of the respective manufacturer.
